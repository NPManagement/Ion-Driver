   Dddaaaaaaaaada sION DRIVER — Controls Reference
================================

KEYBOARD
--------
W / Up Arrow    — Accelerate
S / Down Arrow  — Brake / Reverse
A / Left Arrow  — Steer Left
D / Right Arrow — Steer Right
Space           — Ion Boost (hold)
Q / LMB         — Air Bank Left   (sharp left corner assist)
E / RMB         — Air Bank Right  (sharp right corner assist)
Shift           — Under Boost     (lateral thrust while banking)
Escape          — Pause

GAMEPAD (Xbox/PS layout)
------------------------
Right Trigger   — Accelerate
Left Trigger    — Brake
Left Stick      — Steer
A / Cross       — Ion Boost
LB / L1         — Air Brake Left
RB / R1         — Air Brake Right
Start / Options — Pause

HOW TO OPEN IN GODOT
---------------------
1. Launch Godot_v4.6.1-stable_win64.exe
2. Click "Import"
3. Navigate to Desktop → Ion Driver → project.godot
4. Click "Import & Edit"
5. Press F5 (Play) to run the game

TIPS
----
- Hold Q/E or LMB/RMB air banks in combination with steering for sharper corners
- Boost is most effective on long straights — save energy for them
- Your shield regenerates after 3 seconds without damage
- Boost energy refills automatically but depletes fast — manage it!
- At high speed the camera FOV widens for an intense feel
